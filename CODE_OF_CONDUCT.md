# Conduct

There is no formal code of conduct.

This is a software project. Contributions are judged on technical merit.

**Do not be a dick to people for no reason.** That is the whole policy.

No identity theater, no speech codes, no pronoun mandates, no corporate HR checklist. Disagreement about code, design, and priorities is normal and expected. Harassment, spam, and bad-faith abuse of the tracker or people can still get you blocked — not because of a diversity pledge, but because it wastes time and breaks collaboration.
